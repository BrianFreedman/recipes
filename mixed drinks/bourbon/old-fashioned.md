# Old-Fashioned #

Source: Cocktails: The Art of Mixing in 1,001 Recipes

## Ingredients ##
* 60 ml (2 oz) bourbon
* 1 sugar cube
* 15 ml (1 tsp) water
* 2 dashes orange bitters
* Slice of orange

## Instructions ##
1. Muddle the sugar cube, water, bitters, and orange in a chilled old-fashioned glass.
1. Add 4-5 ice cubes and pour in the bourbon
