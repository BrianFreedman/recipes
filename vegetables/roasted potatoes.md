# Roasted Potatoes #

## Ingredients ##
* 0.908 kg (2 lb) baby potatoes, quartered
* 10 ml (2 tsp) garlic salt
* 30 ml (2 tbsp) neutral oil
* pepper

## Instructions ##
1. Preheat oven to 246 C (475 F).
1. Add potatoes, garlic salt, and oil to a bowl, then toss to coat.
1. Add potatoes to two baking sheets and roast in the top and middle of the oven, flipping potatoes and rotating sheets halfway through, until tender and golden-brown, 20-22 min.
