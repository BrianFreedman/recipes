# Roasted Vegetables #

## Ingredients ##
* 1 onion, chopped
* 2 bell peppers, chopped
* 4 cloves garlic, minced
* 30 ml (2 tbsp) neutral oil
* salt
* pepper

## Instructions ##
1. Preheat oven to 246 C (475 F).
1. Add onion, peppers, garlic, and oil to a bowl. Season with salt and pepper, then toss to coat.
1. Empty bowl onto a baking sheet and roast in the bottom of the oven, until the veggies are tender, 8-12 min.
