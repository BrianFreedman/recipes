# Turmeric Rice #

Source: [Turmeric Rice](https://www.indianhealthyrecipes.com/turmeric-rice/)

## Ingredients ##
* 15 ml (1 tbsp butter)
* 360 ml (1.5 cups) rice
* 540 ml (2.25 cups) chicken broth
* 2.5 ml (0.5 tsp) garlic powder
* 2.5 ml (0.5 tsp) ginger powder
* 2.5 ml (0.5 tsp) turmeric powder
* 2.5 ml (0.5 tsp) salt
* 2.5 ml (0.5 tsp) pepper
* 15 ml (1 tbsp) parsley flakes

## Instructions ##
1. In a pot over medium heat, melt the butter.
1. Add the rice, chicken broth, garlic, ginger, turmeric, salt, pepper, and parsley flakes.
1. Bring the mixture to a boil, and reduce the heat to low. Simmer and cover the pot. Cook on low for 15 minutes.
