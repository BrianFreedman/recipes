# Semi-Homemade Panang Curry Paste #

Source: [Panang Curry](https://hot-thai-kitchen.com/panang-curry/)

## Ingredients ##
* 60 ml (4 tbsp) red curry paste
* 3.75 ml (0.75 tsp) cumin seeds
* 7.5 ml (1.5 tsp) coriander seeds
* 30 ml (2 tbsp) roasted peanuts
* 5 ml (1 tsp) fermented shrimp paste [*optional*]

## Instructions ##
1. In a dry skillet toast the cumin seeds over medium heat until they darken slightly and are aromatic and start to pop, 2-3 minutes; remove from the pan. Repeat with the coriander seeds.
1. Grind the cumin and coriander seeds until very fine using a mortar and pestle or a coffee grinder.
1. Add the roasted peanuts and grind until fine.
1. Mix ground spices into the red curry paste and shrimp paste, if using a mortar and pestle, simply add the paste into the mortar and pound to mix.
