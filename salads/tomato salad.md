# Tomato Salad #

## Ingredients ##
* 15 ml (1 tbsp) lemon juice
* 15 ml (1 tbsp) neutral oil
* 2.5 ml (0.5 tsp) sugar
* 340 g (12 oz) cherry tomatoes, halved <sup>1</sup>
* parsley, chopped <sup>2</sup>
* salt
* pepper

### Substitutions ###
* <sup>1</sup> 4 Roma tomatoes, chopped
* <sup>2</sup> dried parsley, rehydrated

## Instructions ##
1. Whisk together lemon juice, oil, and sugar in a medium bowl
1. Add tomatoes and parsley. Season with salt and pepper, then stir to combine.
