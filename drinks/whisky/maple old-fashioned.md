# Maple Old-Fashioned

Source: LCBO

# Ingredients
* 45 ml (1 1/2 oz) whisky
* 1/2 lemon wheel
* 1/2 orange wheel
* 15 ml (1/2 oz) maple syrup
* 1 dash Angostura bitters

# Instructions
1. To a collins glass, add lemon, orange, and maple syrup.
2. Muddle gently.
3. Fill glass with ice.
4. Add whisky and bitters.
5. Stir.
